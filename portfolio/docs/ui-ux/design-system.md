Be responsive.

Use semantic HTML.

Remain accessible.

Avoid inline styles.

Avoid duplicated styles.

# Design Philosophy

Consistency over creativity.

Simple over clever.

Reusable over duplicated.

Accessibility over aesthetics.

Performance over excessive animation.
